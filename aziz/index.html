hunter x hunter

Gon's Quest Begins:

Gon, raised on Whale Island, discovers his dad is a Hunter (elite license holders). He sets off to become a Hunter himself and find his father.

Hunter Exam:

Gon faces grueling challenges alongside Killua (assassin family heir). He's injured but earns his Hunter license despite Killua's forced withdrawal.

Rescuing Killua:

Gon learns Killua's family brainwashed him and heads to their mansion. Killua defies his family and escapes with Gon, their friendship solidified.

Learning Nen:

Seeking money for his search, Gon and Killua enter a fighting tournament. They discover Nen, a powerful aura system Hunters use, and master it quickly. Gon confronts Hisoka, a dangerous Hunter from the exam, but loses their fight.

A Father's Message:

Back on Whale Island, Gon receives a memento from his father. It reveals a cryptic message urging Gon to "catch" him, a challenge for a fellow Hunter.
